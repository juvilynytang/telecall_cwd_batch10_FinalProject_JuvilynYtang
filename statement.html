Statement of Faith

We believe that the Bible was composed of 39 Old Testament book and 27 New Testament books. It was written with people guided by the Holy spirit, authored by the Lord and salvation is the end. It is our supreme authority in all matters of faith, doctrine, and conduct.

--Isaiah 40:8, John 10:35, 2 Timothy 3:16-17--

We believe that there is only one God that is eternally existent in three persons- Father, Son and Holy Spirit. He is the Creator of Heaven and Earth.

--Deuteronomy 6:4--

We believe that every man was created Holy but choose to disobey God which caused sin to mankind

--Genesis 1:26, Genesis 5:1--

We believe, that salvation of the sinners is only through true faith in Jesus Christ, Son of God, and only those who have faith have assurance of the forgiveness of their sin, including the promise of eternal life, not because of their good-works so one one shall boast. Sinners must be Born again by Holy Spirit, and the results are forgiveness, faith, and new life with God.

--Ephesians 2:8-9, Acts 16:31, Acts 4:12--

We believe that Christian Baptism for a new Christian is through water immersion in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, and the newly baptized Christian could partake in remembering the Last supper of the Lord and His sacrifices.

--Matthew 3:6, 3:13-17, 28:19, 1 Corinthians 11:17-34--

We believe that Sunday is Lord's day.

--Acts 20:7, 1 Corinthians 16:2--

We believe that it is God's will and command for every Christian to be actively engaged in telling others how to establish a personal relationship with God through faith in Jesus Christ and in discipling those who respond to this Good News of salvation.

--Mathhew 28:19-20--

We believe that marriage is the uniting of one man and one woman in a single, exclusive union, as delineated in Scripture. We believe that God intends sexual intimacy to occur only between man and woman who are married to each other.

--Genesis 2:18-25, 1 Corinthians 6:18, 7:2-5, Hebrews 13:4--

We believe that God wonderfully and immutably creates each person as biologically male and female. These two distinct, complementary genders together reflect the image and nature of God. Rejection of one's biological sex is a rejection of the image of God within that person.

--Genesis 1:26-27--

We believe that the second coming of Jesus is near, and on end days, Jesus Christ shall go down from heaven, He will raise the dead which has been forgiven, and will snatch those who are living faithfully with God will go with him.

--1 Thessalonian 4:15-17, 1 Thessalonian 5:2--
