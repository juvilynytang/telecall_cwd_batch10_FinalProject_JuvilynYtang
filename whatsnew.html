WHAT'S NEW!

Alpla Philippines Inc. share the Good News to the children!

    Last Oct 26, 2023 Alpla Philippines Inc. organized a children's community service event lead by GECC member Juvy Ytang that was both heartwarming and impactful. Young minds filled with enthusiasm and compassion came together to make a difference in our community.

During this event, children engaged in various activities like interacting with "Wordless Book" story telling and dancing. It was incredible to witness their energy and dedication as they actively participated in activites, often with smiles on their faces.

Thank God it's Sunday!

     Sundays are a special day for our congregation, as we come together to worship and honor the one we know as 'Jehovah Jireh,' which means 'The Lord Will Provide.' This day is a time of deep reflection and gratitude for the many ways in which our faith sustains us and provides for our needs.

Sunday service is a joyful celebration of the unwavering belief that, just as in the biblical story of Abraham and Isaac, our faith in Jehovah Jireh ensures that our needs are met, our burdens are lightened, and our spirits are lifted. It is a day of unity, prayer, and the recognition that we are blessed beyond measure. In the presence of Jehovah Jireh, we find strength and assurance, knowing that we are cared for and guided on our spiritual journey.

Upcoming Activities!

Touching Lives, Saving Souls: Medical Mission

In collaboration with our dedicated church members and healthcare professionals, we embark on a journey to provide essential medical services this coming February 2024 to underserved communities. Our mission planning encompasses a range of critical activities, including recruiting medical and non-medical volunteers, sourcing medical supplies, and choosing a location where the need is most urgent.

Our approach to this mission extends beyond mere healthcare; it is about holistic care. We are guided by our faith in the importance of health education, patient support, and cultural sensitivity. We ensure that every individual we encounter is treated with the respect, dignity, and love they deserve.

Through this mission, we aim to offer not only medical assistance but also spiritual and emotional support, recognizing that the healing of the body is often intricately connected with the well-being of the soul. Our commitment to prayer, reflection, and spiritual guidance forms an integral part of this mission, as we strive to make a meaningful impact in the lives of those we serve.
